We empower communities around the world
to monitor their environment.





Part of the City Scanner project, Flatburn is an open-source, 3D printed environmental sensor.

The autonomous device is power using a solar panel and can be mounted on vehicles using magnetic pads.

The core module houses the PCB, antenna, and data storage board.

The sensing module houses heat, noise and air quality sensors..

Build your own Flatburn.

Help your community monitor its environment.
