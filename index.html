We empower communities around the world
to monitor their environment.

Part of the City Scanner project, Flatburn is an open-source environmental sensor that can be mounted on vehicles.























This autonomous device is powered using a solar panel and is mounted on vehicles using magnetic pads.























Flatburn is 3D printed.























The core module houses the PCB, antenna, and data storage board.























The sensing module houses heat, noise and air quality sensors..
